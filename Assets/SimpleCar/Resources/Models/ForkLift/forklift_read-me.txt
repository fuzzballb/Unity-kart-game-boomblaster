--------------------forklift; Read Me-------------------------

//Contents.
one very beat up forklift.
Model is in its native .MAX format. Textures are in .TGA format, and include diffuse,specular and normal maps.
Model is free for non-commercial use only, and are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-Non-commercial-No Derivative Works 3.0 License.

//Details.
the forklift is not rigged. the model is subdivided up into seperate elements within the mesh, so will not be hard to export in parts for animation through scripting, or to rig with bones for animation.
